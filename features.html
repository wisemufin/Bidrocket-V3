EVENT MANAGEMENT SOLUTIONS

Manage multiple events for one fundraiser

Create a customized event website where guests can register and buy tickets. Highlight your sponsors and key donors to give them prime advertising space on your home page. You can even open your event early for pre-bidding, generating more dollars for your cause.

FUNDRAISING

Product listings to help raise funds

Create multiple listings for auction items, donation collections, and more using our fast and easy online bidding software. Categorize items, add images, titles, descriptions, and pricing so that guests can easily see and bid on these items before and during events.

REGISTRATION WEBSITE

Share your event with the world

Create your own custom branded registration website where guests can register and buy tickets for multiple events and see before the event starts. Highlight sponsors and key donors with brand logos and encourage others to show support.

Manage your guests, sponsors, donors, and event team with ease.

Gain insights with important analytics and reports

GUEST SEATING

Upgrade to our Guest Seating Management tool to organize and seat guests at assigned tables or seats. You can also customize the arrangement of tables, bars, stages and other elements at the venue.
